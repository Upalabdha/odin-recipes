Chilli Chicken

Home

Description

Chilli Chicken is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made by marinating chicken pieces in soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and cornstarch, then deep-frying them until crispy. The fried chicken is then tossed in a spicy, tangy sauce made with green chilies, garlic, onions, capsicum, soy sauce, vinegar, and a hint of sugar. It’s typically garnished with spring onions and served hot as a starter or with fried rice or noodles.

Ingredients :

  • For Marination :
    • 250g boneless chicken (cut into small cubes)
    • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 tbsp cornflour
    • Salt & Paper
  • For Sauce :
    • Oil
    • Chopped Garlic
    • Chopped Ginger
    • 2-3 Green Chillies
    • 1 small Onion
    • 1 Small Capsicum
    • 1 TableSpoon Vinegar
    • 1 TableSpoon SoyaSauce

Steps :

  1. Marinate the chicken with soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, cornflour, flour, salt, and pepper, and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry the marinated chicken pieces until golden and crispy, then set them aside.
  3. Sauté chopped garlic, ginger, green chilies, onion, and capsicum in a little oil until slightly softened.
  4. Add sauces like soy, chili sauce, vinegar, ketchup, sugar, and salt, then mix in a cornflour slurry to thicken the sauce.
  5. Toss in the fried chicken pieces, coat them well in the sauce, garnish with spring onions, and serve hot.
