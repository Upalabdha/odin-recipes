Description :

Butter Chicken, or Murgh Makhani, is a rich, creamy, and mildly spiced North Indian curry where tender chicken pieces are simmered in a buttery tomato-based sauce. It's a comforting, indulgent dish often enjoyed with naan or basmati rice.

Ingredients :

  • Boneless Chicken
  • Yoghurt
  • Tomato Puree'
  • Butter
  • Fresh Cream

Steps :

  1. Marinate the Chicken in yoghurt , lemon juice and red chilli powder for an hour
  2. Pan Fry the Marinated Chicken
