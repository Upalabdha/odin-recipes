Description :

Biryani is a rich and aromatic rice dish layered with tender marinated meat or vegetables, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of warm spices. Originating from the Indian subcontinent, itâ€™s a celebration of flavor, color, and culture, often slow-cooked for deep infusion.

Ingredients :

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken
  • Yoghurt
  • Onions
  • Biriyani Masala

Steps :

  1. Marinate the pieces with yoghurt , spices and ginger paste
  2. Cook the rice with the spices 70-80% then drain
  3. Saute Onions unitl golden brown and cook chicken until tender
  4. Layer rice and meat in a pot, sprinkle fried onions, mint, and saffron milk.
  5. Keep on low flame for 20 to 25 mins. Let rest before serving.
